Killer Stew

Description:

This stew is so good that it will kill you. No I am not joking. Okay, I am joking. This is just potato stew.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes (any kind)
  • Veggies (carrots, celery, onion)
  • Frozen peas
  • Garlic
  • Dijon mustard
  • Soy sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Herbs (Italian seasoning, rosemary, thyme)
  • Low-sodium vegetable broth or water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Recipe Steps:

  1. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil for 5 minutes. Stir in the diced carrots and celery.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Stir in broth, toss to combine and bring to the boil.
  4. Cook gently, and after 25 minutes add the frozen peas.
  5. Let it simmer for 3 minutes until tender, taste, and adjust the seasoning.
  6. Serve with freshly ground black pepper and crusty bread. Enjoy!
